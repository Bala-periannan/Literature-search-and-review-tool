How It Works

  • Choose a source (e.g., PubMed, CrossRef).
  • Enter your keyword, DOI, or topic.
  • Apply filters like date or status if needed.
  • Click Search to see results with abstracts and citations.
  • Review your History for past searches.

Why Use Lixplore?

  • 100% free and open source.
  • Unified search across multiple databases.
  • Export ready-to-use citations.
  • Seamless Zotero integration
  • Global Search Log.
  • One click, no login hassle.
